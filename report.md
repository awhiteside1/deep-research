## What “city proper” means in France
This point is critical because French administrative geography can be confusing if interpreted through an Anglophone lens.

### Commune vs. urban area vs. metro area
- **Commune**: the basic administrative municipality. This is the closest equivalent to “city proper” for ranking French cities by official population.
- **Population municipale**: INSEE’s official municipal population count for a commune.
- **Population totale**: a broader total population concept that can include other components.
- **Unité urbaine** (urban unit): a built-up area, often crossing commune boundaries.
- **Aire d’attraction / metropolitan area**: a broader commuting-based functional urban area.

Since you specified **city proper**, the relevant population is the **commune population municipale**, not the larger urban or metropolitan area.
